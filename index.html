Welcome! This is me!

Who am I?

My name is Yankun Zhu I was born in 1993 in China. Moved to England when I was seven and I am currently living with my boyfriend in Halifax.

Favourite Creation

It is hard to pick my favourite as I feel proud about every single one of them. Most of my creations were made for family and friends so they all have special meanings to me. However, if I had to pick one it would be the totoro hat as it was my first crochet project.

How did it all begin?

Since I was little, I was always interested in Origami. I would make 2D origamis and from there I stepped into the 3D origami world that to this day still amazes me. From there, I tried to do embroiderys for special occasions and found it intriguing and relaxing. Finally, in recent years I decided to throw myself into crocheting. It has been challenging but also very rewarding.

Future Goals

I hope to improve my skills and venture out into harder and more complex projects as I enjoy the challenge. Someday if the possibility came up I would love to do this fulltime even though I enjoy my current job as a early years practioner.
